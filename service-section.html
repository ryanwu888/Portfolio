Giving Back To The Community

My community in high school was where I developed my interest in engineering. Through classes like Intro to Engineering and Digital Electronics, I was able to think and act like a engineer. Over the summer, I go back to my high school, which hosts summer classes for people like me who want to learn more about engineering, and volunteer to help out those kids. I teach them the basics of engineering and the mindset in order to grow their interests and prepare them for what they need to know in the future. In the future, I would like to give back to Texas A&M University for all it has provided to me to develop my knowledge and capabilities in engineering. I may consider becoming a tutor so that I can teach those who are struggling on their classes. I would also consider doing peer tutoring as it is more open ended to anything a student may have, such as a quick question or two.
